- ## Coastal Management --- A Systems Approach
	- Coastal systems are complex, and in order to be managed in a sustainable way a holistic approach is needed. Because of this IZCM was introduced in Europe in the 1990s.
	- In a review of existing ICZM case studies some weaknesses were observed. Common **weaknesses** include:
	  * Lack of a holistic approach leading to one-sectorial solutions
	  * Late and unbalanced stakeholder involvement leading to public protest
	  * Long durations for the recognition of a problem to the implementation of a solution --- often decades. Now, increasing environmental pressures demand faster action.
	- The **systems approach framework (SAF)** is a *holistic approach* based on systems-thinking (looking at the whole system as opposed to single pieces of it).
