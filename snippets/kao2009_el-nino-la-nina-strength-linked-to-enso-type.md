# kao2009_el-nino-la-nina-strength-linked-to-enso-type

The EP type of ENSO tends to appear more ofren as strong El Niño events than as strong La Niña events. On the other hand, the CP type of ENSO tends to appear more often as strong La Niña events than as strong El Niño events. This reflects the well-known spatial asymmetry between the strong El Niño and strong La Niña events [@Kao2009].
